About Batuk Bhairav Puja

Batuk Bhairav is the child form of Lord Shiva, innocent yet all-powerful. He appears as a small boy — playful to his devotees but terrifying to negativity. While Kaal Bhairav controls time and karma, Batuk Bhairav offers protection, success, health, and spiritual power with the purity of a child and the strength of Mahadev. In Scriptures Shiva Purana calls him the fierce energy of Shiva, created to destroy ego and protect dharma. Tantra texts call him the undefeatable Rudra in child form — joyful, fearless, and full of divine grace. He is easily pleased, but must be approached with faith and sincerity. He blesses swiftly and protects fiercely.

Benefits of the Puja

Protection from Evil Forces

Batuk Bhairav is the destroyer of black magic, ghost attacks, and evil eyes. Many people report feeling lighter, calmer, and mentally clearer just days after the puja.

Legal and Property Matters

He is often worshipped to win legal battles, court cases, and disputes related to land or inheritance.

Career and Business Growth

Batuk Bhairav, when worshipped with oil and mustard seeds, blesses the devotee with steady income, promotions, and business stability.

Health and Healing

Many come here after failing to get results from modern treatment. Batuk Bhairav's blessings are known to heal stubborn illnesses and remove disease energies.
