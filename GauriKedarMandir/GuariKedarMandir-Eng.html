Importance of Mandir

Gaurikedeshwar Temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva located in Kashi. It is worshipped in the Kedarnath form of Lord Shiva. By worshipping here, devotees get relief from all the troubles of life and their virtue increases. It is believed that by worshipping in this temple, devotees get seven times more virtue than Kedarnath Dham. This temple is one of the major pilgrimage sites of Kashi and worshipping here gives spiritual peace and prosperity

Benefits of Puja

Relief from suffering

Worshiping Lord Shiva in Kedarnath Temple brings relief from all kinds of sufferings, mental stress, and physical illnesses in life.

attainment of virtue

By visiting and worshipping in this temple, devotees receive seven times more virtuous results, which makes them experience spiritual peace and happiness.

Salvation and deliverance

Worshiping at Kedarnath Temple eradicates all types of sins from a person's life, and he attains salvation.

Wealth and Prosperity

Worshiping Lord Shiva brings happiness and prosperity in the home and family. This worship is helpful in solving financial problems.
