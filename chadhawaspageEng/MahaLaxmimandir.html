Durga Devi Temple (Durgakund)

Durga Devi Temple, also known as Durgakund Temple, is a famous and sacred religious site in Varanasi. This temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga, who is regarded as the deity of strength and courage. The nearby pond (Durgakund) holds historical and religious significance. During Navratri, a large number of devotees visit the temple to seek blessings and fulfill their wishes by making offerings to the Goddess.

Location: Durgakund, Varanasi
