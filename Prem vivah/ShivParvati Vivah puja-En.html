Understanding Puja in Vedic Tradition

According to our ancient Vedas, Puja is a sacred act of showing reverence to the divine through ritual worship. The word 'Puja' originates from Sanskrit, meaning 'worship' or 'adoration'. It is described in the Vedas as one of the most powerful means to connect with the divine consciousness.

  • Puja involves the offering of devotion (bhakti) through mantras, flowers, incense, and other sacred items
  • It is described in the Upanishads as a means to purify one's mind and attain spiritual elevation
  • The Vedas emphasize that sincere devotion during puja can fulfill both material and spiritual aspirations
